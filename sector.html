Sectors

QDATAMS’ capacity in research project planning, large scale data collection, research project management and reporting span wide range of subjects, bringing insight to topics including marketing, education, economics, global development, health, and public affairs. We have teams across various African countries including Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, Sudan, Democratic Republic of Congo, Somalia, Somali-land, Zambia, Malawi, Zimbabwe and Ethiopia.

We support various sectors including;

QDATAMS has expertise at gathering the data and developing measures to assess market products, economy and individuals’ professional and financial health.

  • Food Security
  • Income and Wealth

EDUCATION, TRAINING, AND LEARNING
